Experience the thrill of the wild and the rich flavors of Africa, with a touch of Safari Sweats!


Once Upon a Time, In the heart of Africa, there was a magical land where the sun set like a golden orb, casting a warm glow over the endless savannas and ancient acacia trees. This was the land of Safari Sweets, a mystical online shop that had been delighting customers since 1987 with its exquisite chocolates and sweets inspired by the African wilderness. But Safari Sweets was more than just a chocolate and sweet shop. It was also a proud supporter of local communities and a champion of sustainable practices. We worked closely with farmers and artisans across Africa to source the finest ingredients, ensuring that our products were not only delicious but also responsibly sourced. Enter our world, where dreams are made of chocolate, and indulge in the rich, velvety flavors that have captivated chocolate lovers for generations. We look forward to sharing our passion for chocolate with you and providing you with an unforgettable chocolate!
